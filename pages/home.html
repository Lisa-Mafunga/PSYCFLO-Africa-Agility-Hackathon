Q1: What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, affecting how we think, feel, and act.

Q2: What are common mental health issues?

Common mental health issues include anxiety, depression, trauma, stress, and mood disorders.

Q3: How can I maintain good mental health?

Maintaining good mental health involves self-care, exercise, healthy eating, social connections, and seeking professional help when needed.

Q4: What are the signs of anxiety?

Signs of anxiety include excessive worry, fear, restlessness, insomnia, and physical symptoms like rapid heartbeat and sweating.
