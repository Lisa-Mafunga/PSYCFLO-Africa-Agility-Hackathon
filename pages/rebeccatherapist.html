Bio

Dr. Rebecca C. James is a licensed marriage and family therapist with over 15 years of experience supporting women through abuse recovery, anxiety, and spiritual trauma.

As a devout Christian, she integrates prayer, Scripture, and holistic wellness into therapy when desired. Her approach is empowering, gentle, and deeply rooted in compassion and evidence-based therapy models.

Specialization

  • Religious Trauma & Spiritual Abuse
  • Anxiety and Panic Disorders
  • Sexual and Domestic Abuse in Women
  • Depression in Young & Midlife Women
  • Marriage Counselling
  • Christian Counselling and Faith Integration
