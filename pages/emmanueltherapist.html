Bio

Emmanuel Chikere, LCSW is a licensed clinical social worker with over 10 years o experience in trauma-focused care, grief counseling, and men's emotional wellness.

With a warm, grounded presence and a practical, results-oriented style, Emmanuel helps individualsnavigate complex emotions, past abuse, and chronic stress

His sessions are rooted in evidence-based methods like CBT, DBT, and solution-focused therapy, tailored to each client's unique journey.
